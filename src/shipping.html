Our fleet and capabilities

Supramax vessels (~50 000 т)

Reliable mid-size workhorses of global trade. Supramax vessels are perfect for ports with depth limits or tidal shifts — agile, self-sufficient, and ready to operate where infrastructure is minimal.

Equipped with four onboard cranes (30–35 t each), they can load or unload cargo even at anchor. Stable and manoeuvrable in tight waters, they’re a great choice for Southeast Asia and beyond.

Why choose Supramax?
Quick port calls, flexible routes, no delays.
Panamax vessels (~75,000 tons)

Big-volume carriers built for global reach.

Panamax vessels are designed to fit the original Panama Canal — and are still vital for moving bulk cargo across continents. With their high capacity and efficiency, they handle major shipments with ease and reliability.

Perfect for long-haul logistics where size, stability, and predictable transit really matter.
